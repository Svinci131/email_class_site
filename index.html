Styling part II:Classes (est 15 min)

  • Go over Classes

    • Inline Styles vs Classes

Testing & Sending/Working with Different Email Clients: (est 20 min)

  • Use a free email previewer(probably: https://my.sendinblue.com) to preview email we've been building
  • Go over the ways different email clients behave
  • Give this example:
    • When we preview our email we'll see it looks strange in Outlook
    • This is because we used margin 0 auto, to center everything which Outlook doesn't recognize
    • We could fix this by removing the margin and adding padding left, but then we’ll have less consistency on mobile device.
    • We could add classes instead of inline styles and add media queries, but sometimes browsers strips out external styles
Final Exercise:

Upload a list(class emails) and send out our email
